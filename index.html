Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

ArsenalDB stores everything locally using your browser's built-in database. There is no server. There is no account. We never see your data — not your firearms, not your serial numbers, not your personal information. Your inventory exists only on the device you're using right now. Back it up, restore it, wipe it — you're in complete control.
