What It Does

Everything a serious
owner needs

ArsenalDB is a full-featured firearm management platform that runs entirely on your device — no account, no cloud, no exposure.

🔫
Firearm Inventory
Log every firearm with make, model, caliber, serial number, condition, purchase price, current value, photos, and storage location.
Core Feature
📋
Bill of Sale Wizard
5-step transfer wizard with seller and buyer info, compliance gates for interstate and NFA items, PDF generation, and auto-archive on completion.
Legal Protection
⬛⬛
QR Transfer Mode
Buyer scans a QR code on their own phone, fills in their details, draws their signature, and receives a completion code. The entire transfer happens in person, instantly.
Differentiator
🔗
Chain of Custody
Every firearm maintains a complete ownership history — auto-logged on creation and transfer. Print a custody record at any time.
Audit Trail
🎯
Range Sessions
Log shooting sessions with round counts, group sizes, drill results, weather conditions, and target photos. Linked directly to your firearms — round totals accumulate automatically.
Performance Tracking
⚙
Maintenance Tracking
Work order system per firearm. Set service intervals by round count and get automatic alerts when a gun is due for cleaning or a spring replacement.
Preventive Care
🔩
Schematics & Parts
AI-generated field strip guides, assembly steps, parts lists, and diagrams for any firearm. Upload your own schematics as a backup.
AI Powered
✦
AI Gun Identifier
Take a photo of any firearm and AI identifies the make, model, type, and caliber instantly. Includes market value estimates and gunsmith diagnostics.
AI Powered
📊
Reports & Export
Insurance reports, theft reports, full inventory exports to CSV and PDF. Everything you need for documentation, coverage, and claims.

How It Works

Up and running
in minutes

1
Open the App
No download required. Open arsenaldb.com in your mobile browser and install as a PWA with one tap.
2
Log Your Firearms
Add each firearm with photos, serial numbers, values, and any notes. Takes about 90 seconds per gun.
3
Manage & Track
Log range sessions, schedule maintenance, generate reports, and handle transfers — all from one place.
🔒
Your data never leaves your device
ArsenalDB stores everything locally in your browser using IndexedDB. There is no server, no account, no sync, and no third party that can access your inventory. You own your data completely.
